About Me

Discover my journey from technology to social work

Background & Journey

I'm a computer science student taking social work as an elective, which puts me in a unique position. Growing up in an immigrant family, I've experienced firsthand the intersection of culture, identity, and systemic barriers that many of our clients face.

My journey into social work began with a recognition that technology alone cannot solve the complex human challenges we face as a society. While I have strong coding skills and technical background, I realized that true innovation comes from understanding the human experience and the systemic forces that shape it.

Unique Perspective

What sets me apart is my ability to bridge the gap between technical solutions and human-centered social work practice. I believe that combining tech and social work can create powerful tools for advocacy, data-driven policy change, and innovative service delivery.

My immigrant background gives me deep empathy for those navigating cultural dislocation and systemic barriers. I understand the pressure to succeed, the fear of uncertainty, and the strength it takes to build a new life in an unfamiliar system.

Career Aspirations

I envision a career where I can:

  • Work directly with clients to provide compassionate, culturally-sensitive care
  • Use my technical skills to improve social work practice and service delivery
  • Advocate for systemic change at both individual and policy levels
  • Bridge the gap between technology and human services
